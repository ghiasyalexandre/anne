Our Story

It's a Forever Thing

In April 2011, Ronnie and Ashley met as a "wingman" and a "third wheel" to their respective best friends. Despite not looking for a relationship, they felt a connection that neither of them had ever felt before. After weeks of talking to each other everyday, sharing stories of past experiences, dreams for the future, and everything in between, they became inseparable.

The two of them start spending more and more time together, eventually discovering that their connection has become far deeper than either of them expected. Ashley and Ronnie started dating, travelled the world together, played numerous escape room games, shared secrets, and enjoyed each other's company. They were in love, and it became undeniable.

Two hearts meant for each other; Ashley and Ronnie are living proof that even the most unlikely of stories can turn into a beautiful romance that lasts a lifetime. Twelve years later, they choose forever. Love is an unexpected journey!
